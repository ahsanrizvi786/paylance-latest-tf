OUR STORY

Finance shouldn't have borders.

Payelance was born from a simple frustration: managing crypto and fiat money across different platforms is broken. You need one app for banking, another for crypto, a third for international transfers. It's slow, expensive, and exhausting.

So we built something better. Payelance by OrbitWalls is the first card that seamlessly unites traditional banking with digital assets. Spend, send, receive, and manage everything from one secure dashboard — whether you're paying in US dollars, Euros, or Ethereum.

Today, thousands of individuals and businesses across 150+ countries trust Payelance to move money freely, without hidden fees or unnecessary delays.
